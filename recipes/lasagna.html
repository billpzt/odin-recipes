Lasagna

Description:

This is a lasagna recipe I got from the internet to practice my webdesign skills. I love lasagnas, they are really tasty. This is a paragraph that describes the lasagna. So, it's a dish with lots of melted cheese, some pasta and some other delicious stuff all layered.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
